EchoPulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

EchoPulse is a B2B churn intelligence platform. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. What data we collect

  • Stripe customer IDs and company names: Used to identify which accounts to monitor and associate signals with the correct customer.
  • News signals from public sources: We query public news sources for relevant events (e.g., layoffs, acquisitions) tied to your customer companies.
  • LinkedIn profile changes via Proxycurl: We may check for job/company changes for key contacts you choose to monitor.

2. How we use your data

  • To monitor churn signals and detect risk patterns early.
  • To send alert emails when a meaningful signal is detected.
  • Never sold to third parties: We do not sell your data.

3. Data security

  • All data encrypted at rest in our storage providers.
  • Supabase Row Level Security is used to restrict access to your records.
  • Keys never stored in plain text: API keys are handled securely and are not stored in plaintext.

4. Your rights

  • Delete your data anytime by requesting deletion.
  • Export your data on request in a reasonable format.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, requests, or concerns, email obinna@echopulse.co.

We aim to collect only what we need to provide churn intelligence and alerts. If you have questions about a specific data field or integration, reach out anytime.
