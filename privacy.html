EchoAssist Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

What We Collect

EchoAssist processes voice transcripts from your microphone during active calls. These transcripts are sent to our AI backend solely to generate real-time coaching suggestions and call summaries.

How We Use Your Data

Transcripts are processed in real time to provide AI coaching suggestions. We do not store, sell, or share your call transcripts with any third party.

Data Retention

Call transcripts are processed and discarded immediately. No call content is stored on our servers.

Third Party Services

EchoAssist uses the Anthropic Claude API to process transcripts. No personally identifiable information is included in API requests.
